Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account? ‚ñº
You can create an account using your email address, Apple ID, or Google account. Simply open the app and tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen. Your data is encrypted and completely secure.
Is EverKind free to use? ‚ñº
EverKind offers both free and premium features. Basic AI coaching and journaling are free, while advanced features like detailed analytics and unlimited chat history require a subscription.
Can I use the app offline? ‚ñº
Yes! You can write journal entries and access previously downloaded content offline. AI coaching features require an internet connection, but your entries will sync when you're back online.

Privacy & Security

Is my data secure and private? ‚ñº
Absolutely. All your conversations and journal entries are encrypted end-to-end. We follow HIPAA-compliant practices and never share your personal information with third parties.
Can you see my conversations? ‚ñº
No. Your conversations are encrypted and only accessible to you. Our AI processes your messages to provide responses, but human staff cannot read your private conversations.
How do I delete my account? ‚ñº
You can delete your account anytime from the Settings page. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers within 30 days.

Using the AI Coach

How does the AI coach work? ‚ñº
Our AI coach uses evidence-based therapeutic techniques including CBT and mindfulness. It learns from your interactions to provide increasingly personalized support while maintaining strict privacy standards.
Can the AI replace therapy? ‚ñº
No. EverKind is designed to complement, not replace, professional mental health care. For serious mental health concerns, please consult with a licensed therapist or counselor.
What if the AI gives harmful advice? ‚ñº
Our AI is trained to be supportive and safe, with built-in safeguards. If you ever receive concerning responses, please report them to us immediately. Always seek professional help for serious concerns.

Technical Support

The app is crashing or won't open ‚ñº
Try force-closing and reopening the app. If that doesn't work, restart your device or reinstall the app. Your data will be preserved if you're logged into your account.
I can't sync my data across devices ‚ñº
Make sure you're logged into the same account on both devices and have a stable internet connection. Data sync may take a few moments on the first login.
Which devices are supported? ‚ñº
EverKind supports iOS 15.1+ on iPhone and iPad. We're working on Android support and will announce availability soon.
